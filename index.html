About Me

Hello, my name is Gisselle Robleto. I am a current Structural Engineering undergraduate student at the University of California, San Diego. The moment I realized I wanted to become a Structural Engineer was when I heard about my uncle helping build schools for underprivileged children in Latin America. Not only was I inspired by his story, but I was also excited for what I could do in the future. Now, I aspire to be part of the Engineers Without Borders organization to help people in areas that have been hit by disasters and calamities. I want to make a change in this world by helping those that are in need and I know I can do it with enough perseverance and dedication.
